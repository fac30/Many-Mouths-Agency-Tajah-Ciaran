About Us

Many Mouths started out with one single mission - to bring stories relegated to the background to the forefront of the literary world. We have been representing award-winning talent for the last 30 years, and have no signs of stopping.

Our Authors

At Many Mouths, we take immense pride in representing a diverse and exceptionally talented group of authors, each with a unique voice and a captivating story to share.

Prepare to be entangled in a web of suspense and intrigue as world-class author Neil Tran unfolds his latest mystery masterpiece, "The Girl In The Woods." Tran, renowned for his literary prowess and ability to weave intricate narratives, invites readers on a riveting journey through the shadowy realms of secrets, deception, and the untold mysteries that lie hidden among the trees.

Prepare to embark on a transformative odyssey through the pages of Jonathan Patterson's debut fiction marvel, "The Journey Of Life Is A Secret." In this compelling narrative, Patterson invites readers to traverse the intricate tapestry of life, where secrets lie hidden, waiting to be unveiled. As an upcoming author, Patterson emerges as a literary tour de force, weaving a tale that captures the essence of human existence and the clandestine threads that bind us all.

Step into the world of heartfelt emotions and timeless love with the enchanting romance novel, "Waiting For You," penned by the amazing author Hannah Morales. In this spellbinding tale, Morales weaves a narrative that transcends time, capturing the essence of love's enduring power and the journey to rediscover one's heart in the most unexpected places.

In the realm of creativity and inspiration, Susan Williams emerges as a guiding force with her latest non-fiction marvel, "Mind Blown: Unleashing Creativity." Through the pages of this transformative work, Willis invites readers on a riveting exploration of the boundless power within every mind—a power waiting to be unlocked, nurtured, and set free.
