About Us

Many Mouths started out with one single mission - to bring stories relegated to the background to the forefront of the literary world. We have been representing award-winning talent for the last 30 years, and have no signs of stopping. We have sicne expanded our roster to include screenwriters.


At Many Mouths, we embark on a journey through the limitless landscapes of storytelling, where every narrative is a thread woven into the rich tapestry of human experience. Established with a fervent passion for literature and a commitment to diverse voices, Many Mouths is not merely a literary agency; it is a vibrant hub where creativity knows no bounds, and stories find their voice.

Our Vision: Celebrating Diversity, Amplifying Voices

Our agency is founded on the belief that within the multitude of voices lies the true richness of our collective story. We strive to be a beacon for diversity, a platform where voices from every background, genre, and perspective are not just represented but celebrated. Many Mouths envisions a literary landscape that reflects the mosaic of the human experience, embracing stories that resonate across cultures, genres, and borders.

Leadership: Emma Harper's Visionary Stewardship

At the helm of Many Mouths is Emma Harper, our Head of Books and a visionary leader in the literary realm. With a profound love for literature and an instinct for exceptional storytelling, Emma guides our agency toward a future where every author's voice is recognized and every story is given the opportunity to thrive. As a literary enthusiast and advocate, Emma ensures that Many Mouths is a dynamic space where authors are not just represented but nurtured.

Agents: Navigators of Literary Exploration

Our team of literary agents, led by the Genre Explorer Lucas Hernandez, is dedicated to navigating the diverse landscapes of storytelling. We understand that literature is an ever-expanding realm with genres as varied as the human imagination. Our agents are more than representatives; they are literary enthusiasts committed to discovering, nurturing, and celebrating the plethora of voices within our agency. Whether you're a seasoned author or an emerging talent, our agents are here to be your literary compass.

Many Mouths, Many Genres: Unleashing Creativity

At Many Mouths, we are Genre Explorers, traversing the vast expanse of literary possibilities. From gripping thrillers to heartwarming romances, thought-provoking non-fiction to epic fantasies, we are dedicated to showcasing the incredible diversity of stories that the world has to offer. Our commitment is to open new doors, break down barriers, and create a space where creativity knows no limits.
