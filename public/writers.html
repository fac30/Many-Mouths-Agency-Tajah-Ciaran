Our Authors

At Many Mouths, we take immense pride in representing a diverse and exceptionally talented group of authors, each with a unique voice and a captivating story to share.

Prepare to be entangled in a web of suspense and intrigue as world-class author Neil Tran unfolds his latest mystery masterpiece, "The Girl In The Woods." Tran, renowned for his literary prowess and ability to weave intricate narratives, invites readers on a riveting journey through the shadowy realms of secrets, deception, and the untold mysteries that lie hidden among the trees.

Prepare to embark on a transformative odyssey through the pages of Jonathan Patterson's debut fiction marvel, "The Journey Of Life Is A Secret." In this compelling narrative, Patterson invites readers to traverse the intricate tapestry of life, where secrets lie hidden, waiting to be unveiled. As an upcoming author, Patterson emerges as a literary tour de force, weaving a tale that captures the essence of human existence and the clandestine threads that bind us all.

Step into the world of heartfelt emotions and timeless love with the enchanting romance novel, "Waiting For You," penned by the amazing author Hannah Morales. In this spellbinding tale, Morales weaves a narrative that transcends time, capturing the essence of love's enduring power and the journey to rediscover one's heart in the most unexpected places.

In the realm of creativity and inspiration, Susan Williams emerges as a guiding force with her latest non-fiction marvel, "Mind Blown: Unleashing Creativity." Through the pages of this transformative work, Willis invites readers on a riveting exploration of the boundless power within every mind—a power waiting to be unlocked, nurtured, and set free.

In the enchanting realm of "Forest Journey," the written tapestry of nature comes alive under the penmanship of the talented author, Aaron Ioeb. This non-fiction odyssey invites readers to embark on a transformative exploration of the world's vibrant forests, uncovering the untold stories woven into the heart of these majestic landscapes.

Prepare to be engulfed in a chilling tale that transcends the boundaries of reality and nightmares. "In Your Eyes," penned by the brilliant author Margarita Perez, is a spine-tingling journey into the supernatural, where the shadows whisper secrets, and fear lurks just beyond the edge of perception.

Prepare to embark on a spellbinding journey into the realms of mystery and magic with the incredible new work from our talented author, Olivia Wilson, titled "Soul." In this captivating fiction tale, Wilson weaves a narrative that transcends the ordinary, inviting readers into a world where the mystical and the mundane converge in an intricate dance of enchantment.

In the hallowed halls of literary excellence, Samuel Reita once again enchants readers with his latest masterpiece, the legacy of the whispering blade with the finesse of a seasoned storyteller, Reita unveils a riveting non-fiction saga that transcends time, transporting readers to a realm where a single sword weaves a tapestry of legend and destiny.

In a world often defined by excess and extremes, "Lagom" serves as a compass guiding readers toward a more harmonious existence. Wilson artfully combines personal anecdotes, cultural exploration, and expert insights to unravel the nuanced tapestry of lagom, a concept that transcends mere moderation. It is a philosophy that advocates for the perfect equilibrium between work and play, solitude and sociality, simplicity and complexity.

In the literary realm, Steffan Wienburg emerges as a luminary storyteller, weaving a mesmerizing narrative in his latest non-fiction masterpiece, "The Story of the Tree." With unparalleled finesse, Wienburg breathes life into the silent guardians of our planet, uncovering the profound wisdom that lies within the majestic world of trees.

At Many Mouths, we believe in the power of storytelling to connect, inspire, and transform. Our vision is to create a literary haven where diversity reigns supreme, and every narrative, whether whispered or shouted, is given the platform it deserves. We understand that within the multitude of voices lies the richness of our collective human story.


Beyond the business of books, Many Mouths is a community—a gathering place for authors, readers, and literary enthusiasts to connect, collaborate, and celebrate the magic of storytelling. Join us in exploring the vibrant world of literature, where every voice contributes to the symphony of human expression.
